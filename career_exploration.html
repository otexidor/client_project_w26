Career Exploration FAQ

  • How can I explore different career options within Information Sciences?
    Career exploration is an ongoing process. Students are encouraged to take a variety of courses, attend employer events and career panels, connect with alumni, and participate in internships or research opportunities. Visit the CDO canvas site for more information.
  • How can the Career Development Office support Information Sciences students?
    The Career Development Office offers career advising, resume and portfolio reviews, interview preparation, employer events, and resources to support students at every stage of career exploration.
  • Is it okay to include coursework or student projects in my application materials?
    Absolutely! UMSI coursework and projects are valued by employers, especially if they show relevant skills and impact.
  • Where do I begin?
    A great place to start is by booking an appointment with a career coach for 1:1 guidance.

Connect with the UMSI Career Development Office

The UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) offers personalized support through appointments, events, and resource guides. Visit the CDO website or find us on campus to learn more, schedule a coaching session, or just say hello.
